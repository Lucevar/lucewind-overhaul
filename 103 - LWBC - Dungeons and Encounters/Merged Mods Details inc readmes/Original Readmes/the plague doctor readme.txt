The plague doctor a dark alchemist of ill repute and complex motives has come to vvardenfell and brought his morbid experimentations with him. he hides away in a new cave within the bitter coast. Though a tough opponent his drip is exceptional, defeat him and acquire it for yourself but be warned, this is one mastermind from which there is Zero Escape.

made for modathon 2023

Requires OAAB DATA

Special thanks to Katya for making the cave.


REQUIREMENTS:
This mod uses textures from the official lefemme plugin, they usually come with most modern versions of the game but if you don't have it you can get it from the nexus or just use enhanced textures/Intelligent textures which contain the upscaled versions of those textures.
